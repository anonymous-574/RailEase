Welcome to RailEase !! Your One-Stop Solution for Seamless Train Travel


RailEase is a user-friendly online platform designed to simplify the process of train ticket booking and management. With just a few clicks, you can easily search for trains, select your preferred class and date, and book your tickets.



Key Features of RailEase:

  • User-Friendly Interface: A simple and intuitive interface makes the booking process hassle-free.
  • Real-Time Availability: Access to real-time train schedules and seat availability.
  • Secure Payment Gateway: Safe and secure payment options for hassle-free transactions.
  • PNR Status Tracking: Track the status of your booked tickets in real-time.
  • Easy Cancellation and Refunds: A smooth process for cancellations and refunds.
  • Personalized Preferences: Save your preferences for faster future bookings.
By digitizing the railway reservation system, RailEase aims to enhance the overall travel experience, reduce waiting times, and contribute to the growth of the digital India initiative.
